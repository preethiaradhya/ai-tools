🎯 Quick Practice Exercises — Try These Right Now!
Exercise 1 — ChatGPT
Open ChatGPT and type: "Explain artificial intelligence to a 10th grade student in India using examples from cricket and farming." See how detailed the answer is.
Exercise 2 — Gemini
Open Gemini and ask: "What are the top 5 AI jobs in India in 2025 and what skills do they need?" Notice how it uses current web information.
Exercise 3 — Canva AI
Open Canva AI Image Generator and type: "A student in South India learning technology on a laptop, bright colors, inspirational" — see what it creates!
Exercise 4 — Bhashini
Open Bhashini and translate this sentence to your regional language: "Artificial Intelligence is changing the world and creating new opportunities for everyone."
Exercise 5 — Programiz
Open Programiz Python IDE and type: print("Hello, I am learning AI for ALL at ROOMAN!") and click Run. You just ran your first Python code!
Exercise 6 — Compare Two Tools
Ask the same question in both ChatGPT and Claude: "What are 3 ways AI can help small businesses in Tier 2 cities of India?" Compare their answers.
